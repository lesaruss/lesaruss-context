The GeekFon Society structure

Each brand under LESARUSS runs on a defined structure of roles, not a list of names. For GeekFon Society, three role types carry the brand:

  • Sub-Label Heads Run the genre layer. One per sub-label (Shamanic Resin, Mad Tings, and the labels still to come). Hold the artists, releases, and the sound.
  • Character Producers Build the animated cast and the music they make. Story, song, and voice all under one role. They keep the universe canon.
  • Society Members The audience that participates as fans, not customers. Vote on releases, request sets on GeekFon Radio, shape what the universe sounds like next.

6. Frequently asked

The questions worth answering.

Is there a deadline? What if we do not hit the threshold?
There is no deadline. The campaign runs continuously until the threshold is met. If we have not hit it yet, the line keeps moving from where it last left off, never resets. We are not running a clock, we are raising what it takes to launch this brand the right way. Two months, two years, ten years, it is what it is. The day we hit the threshold is the day this brand goes fully live.
Will I get refunded if the threshold is never met?
No. Pledges are not held in escrow. The moment you back this brand, your dollars go directly into building it, the studio, the characters, the radio, the next release. By backing, you are funding the build, not waiting on it.
How do I track where my pledge goes?
Once you pledge, a campaign tracker pins to your Mission Control. You see the line move in real time, the milestones the team is hitting, and where the funds are deploying inside the brand. You always know what your dollars did.
What do I get when this brand officially launches?
A founding role inside GeekFon Society, a permanent access tile in your Mission Control, GeekFon Radio in your sidebar, and the head of the line for whatever drops first. Founding pledges count differently than late ones.
Do I need a separate account for each brand I back?
No. Backing a brand adds it as a tile inside your existing Mission Control. One identity across every brand in the universe.
How is this different from a Kickstarter?
Kickstarter is all-or-nothing on a deadline. We are neither. There is no deadline, every pledge funds the build the day it lands, and backing here puts you inside an operating system that connects this brand to seven others under the same roof.
