# LESARUSS Academy Cartridge

## What This Is
LESARUSS Academy is the training and certification program members go through to become **Creative Producers** for the LESARUSS Universe. A paid product. Graduates receive guaranteed employment across LESARUSS properties. Academy exists so every LESARUSS brand has a steady pipeline of vetted candidates who already operate on the house standard. The hire-from-within production engine.

- Primary URL: `academy.lesaruss.ai` (subdomain convention locked 2026-04-22 for LESARUSS-prefixed brands)
- Stack: Next.js 16, Supabase, Vercel (shared LESARUSS platform)

## The Product
Paid training program + certification. Members enroll, complete the coursework, pass certification, and graduate to employable Creative Producer status inside the LESARUSS Universe.

**Guaranteed employment rule:** anyone who completes and passes the Academy course is guaranteed employment across a LESARUSS property. This is the product's marquee promise, not a marketing line. Capacity is managed through Academy enrollment + Booker sales + touring brand demand, in that order.

Creative Producers feed into:
- Mogul in the Making production crew (UPM cartridge, DP contracts)
- The Blueprint animation team (storyboards, character sheets, shorts production)
- Chester Is Cool + Anime3000 studios
- GeekFon Society radio + podcast production
- Russell's Roving Reporters student-media coaching
- Any new LESARUSS brand that activates and needs a Producer

## Why It Exists
Every LESARUSS property is production-dependent. Without a vetted pipeline, Producers are a bottleneck. Academy turns members into the pipeline. Also compounds: every graduate becomes a case study, a recruiter, and a brand evangelist.

## Certification Track (pattern to build)
- Foundation (universal LESARUSS standards: voice, ADA, Compliance Radar, Console / Cartridge vocabulary, `console/deploy.md`, no em-dashes)
- Production (Line Producer Console fundamentals, Content Pipeline v4.0, Seedance + Suno stack)
- Specialization (choose track: video, audio, animation, live-tour, district/K12)
- Capstone (real production placement on an active LESARUSS property, mentored)
- Certification (passes = guaranteed placement)

## Rules
- Certification is the gate to employment guarantee, not enrollment
- Every Academy graduate must pass the ADA Unlocked accessibility module (universal)
- Employment placements route through Booker Console + touring-brand GMs, Academy does not place directly
- Pricing tier lands inside the LESARUSS Model (see `project_lesaruss_model.md`), Academy is a paid product
- Members-first rollout per `console/compliance.md` Rule 4
