# Chester Is Cool Cartridge

## What This Is
Chester Is Cool is a kids and family media brand inside the LESARUSS Universe. A K12 Unlocked client. Animated shorts, educational content, plant-based and accessibility-forward stories, built on the Seedance 2.0 + Suno animation stack. Waiting room live on April 20, 2026 soft launch.

## Domain
- Primary URL: `chesteriscool.com`
- Sits under K12 Unlocked as a client, also functions as an independent media brand
- Animation production in `/animation/` per animation playbook

## Key People
- **Wren Calloway** — General Manager, Chester Is Cool. Briefings every Wednesday 10 AM.
- **Brandon Cole** — K12 Unlocked GM (Chester's umbrella). Accountability owner.
- **Jasmine Laurent** — Chief Creative Officer. Owns brand voice and visual standard.

## The Product
Animated kids and family content: shorts, comics, music videos, curriculum tie-ins. Sold into schools via K12 Unlocked, consumed directly by families via the Chester brand site and social.

Production stack: Seedance 2.0 (video), Suno (music), Claude + Video Prompt Builder (prompts). OP and ED theme songs for every show. All music feeds the shared LESARUSS waiting room music library. See `playbooks/animation-playbook-2026-04-10.html`.

## Overlap With Other Brands
- **K12 Unlocked** — parent umbrella. Chester is the media brand K12 districts license for student content and curriculum.
- **Russell's Roving Reporters** — sibling K12 client. RRR is the real-kid journalism brand, Chester is the animated family brand. Cross-promote via shared student media showcases.
- **Vegans Explore** — plant-based messaging bridge. Chester teaches kids, VE holds the adult community. Partner campaign candidates.
- **Anime3000** — shared animation production stack. Chester content doubles as proof-of-concept for Anime3000's expanded production ambitions.
- **GeekFon Society** — Chester's music videos live in the GeekFon Radio network and waiting room library.
- **ADA Unlocked** — kids content must be fully accessible, captions, audio descriptions, reduced motion, readable typography.

## Rules
- Always write the full name "Chester Is Cool," never just "Chester"
- Kids-facing content must clear additional content review (no violence, no ad targeting, COPPA-compliant data)
- All video must have captions at publish time, not post-hoc
- Music videos double as teasers for the shared waiting room library, watermark accordingly
- No em-dashes, orange + white brand colors, no dark mode for family-facing surfaces

## How to Apply
When any agent works on Chester content, curriculum tie-ins, or animation production, load this cartridge plus `cartridges/brands/k12-unlocked.md`. For production workflow, reference animation playbook.
