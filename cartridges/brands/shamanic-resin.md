Shamanic Resin is the LESARUSS K-pop music project. Tied directly to the Anime3000 universe: each anime episode debuts a song. Music and character are inseparable, the song belongs to the episode as a release, not as a soundtrack. Completely distinct from Mad Tings (the UK grime/dubstep/reggae brand).
