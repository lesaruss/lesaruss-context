## What B2B Actually Is (clarified Apr 20, 2026)
A full daily reality-show narrative of Sean's life. Not just business. Everything: the drive, the event, the conversation in the parking lot, the music session, the random encounter, the storage unit visit. Nia crafts the raw material into compelling reading. The goal: Sean can return to any day in his life and know exactly what happened.

Reads like a reality show. Nia is the writer/showrunner. Fieldy transcripts are the raw footage.

## B2B Capture Triggers — CRITICAL
When Sean says **"bars"** or **"please take note of this"** during a recording, those are flagged moments. Capture as close to verbatim as possible. They appear in the B2B entry for that day. Sean does this 5 to 7 times daily. These are highlights, the moments he knows are worth preserving. **Never skip them.**

## Workflow (updated Apr 20, 2026)
1. Fieldy captures Sean's day in real time (voice memos + direct transcription)
2. The `v-fieldy-report` scheduled task processes transcripts and produces TWO outputs:
   - **Sean Report** (business layer) — what's happening with the brands
   - **B2B Daily Narrative Digest** (for Nia) — full day story + all "bars" + "please take note" moments flagged and quoted
3. Nia takes the B2B digest and writes the narrative chapter (compelling, reality-show quality)
4. Victoria reviews for brand safety before posting
5. Chapter publishes, Sean is off the approval chain
6. Only flag to Sean if something could genuinely hurt the brand
