# The Blueprint Cartridge

## What This Is
The Blueprint is the flagship scripted animated series inside the LESARUSS Universe. Agency mockumentary set at GeekFon Society. Cross of The Studio and The Office. Franchise Bible v2 locked 2026-04-21.

## Domain
- No standalone domain yet. Distributes via GeekFon Society (animation + music label) and the LESARUSS platform.
- Franchise Bible: `/LESARUSS HQ/playbooks/universe-bible.html`

## The Show
- Animated mockumentary in the GeekFon house style.
- Set at a small brand-building agency that runs an in-universe media label called GeekFon Society and sells "a system to build systems."
- Tone anchors: The Studio meets The Office. Chaos of a creative business plus earnest camera-as-coworker warmth.
- Characters are fictional. The Sean-prime character is a founder archetype, not a portrayal of Sean A. Russell.

## Episode 1
"The Mad Tings Visit." GeekFon Society is hours away from a walkthrough with Mad Tings (UK grime, dubstep, reggae crew), who are deciding whether to sign with the label. Cold open becomes a whole day. They do not say yes, but they do not leave.

Note: Mad Tings in the show is fictionalized. Do not conflate with Shamanic Resin (the K-pop project). See `cartridges/brands/shamanic-resin.md` and `cartridges/brands/mad-tings.md`.

- **Mogul in the Making** — parallel sibling, not overlap. Mogul = live-action reality, Sean-led. Blueprint = animated, scripted, cast-led. Easter eggs and cameos allowed later, structurally separate in Phase 1.
- **From Blur to Blueprint** — origin story the sitcom world sits inside. Careful: the sitcom is "The Blueprint" (distinct from FBTB and from Blueprint to Beyond). Never conflate.
