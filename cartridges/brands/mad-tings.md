# Mad Tings Cartridge

## What This Is
Mad Tings is the LESARUSS UK Grime, Dubstep, and Reggae music project. Completely distinct from Shamanic Resin (K-pop, anime-linked). Different genre, different identity, different audience. Never conflate the two.

Mad Tings is also the premise of The Blueprint (sitcom) Episode 1 — "The Mad Tings Visit" — where the fictional Mad Tings are considering signing with fictional GeekFon Society. The real-brand Mad Tings and in-show Mad Tings are separate. Do not blend.
