# Mogul in the Making Cartridge

## What This Is
Mockumentary reality series documenting Sean A. Russell's journey toward becoming a billionaire. Framed as a Citizen Kane flashback narrative from the perspective of a future billionaire. Primary personal-brand vehicle alongside From Blur to Blueprint. Also the live R&D ground for the Line Producer Console and Booker Console as sellable LESARUSS products.

## Domain
- No standalone domain yet. Lives inside Sean A. Russell brand (`seanarussell.com`) and distributes via the GeekFon Society podcast network and LESARUSS platform.
- Episode 1 target: June 1, 2026

## Production Playbook
v1.2 locked Apr 21, 2026 at `playbooks/mogul-in-the-making-production-playbook.html`. Victoria owns it.

## Role Vocabulary (locked Apr 21, industry-accurate)
- **Sean** = Executive Producer and Producer, consolidated into one Founder Seat. Owner-operator studio model (Tyler Perry, Issa Rae, Donald Glover precedent). This seat does NOT productize.
- **Victoria Egan** = Line Producer, runs the production from V Station. This seat IS a Console (Line Producer Console). Every LESARUSS touring brand loads the same Console with a brand-specific cartridge.
- **UPM** (Unit Production Manager) = on-road operator and on-camera audience surrogate. Replaces earlier "Field Lead" language (deprecated). Rotated per quarter, 90-day max, primary + secondary named. UPM is a cartridge inside the Line Producer Console, not its own Console.
- **Booker** = upstream sales. Marcus Webb on sponsorships. Dedicated Booker for speaking/partnerships not yet named. Booker is its own Console, adjacent to Line Producer Console.
- **DP** = Director of Photography. Contracted per trip, not a Console.

## Revenue Model
$25K floor per trip, non-negotiable. Covers Line Producer + UPM + DP + travel + post + margin. Secondary targets required before green-light: relationships closed, speaking advanced to proposal, content units produced, sponsor conversations scheduled. Miss 3 of 4 and it is a warning trip, not a win.

## Trip Cadence
14-day pre-trip runway, 72 hours on ground, 7-day debrief. Footage handoff to Edit within 48 hours. Tied to Content Pipeline Playbook v4.0.

## "LESARUSS" Character
The on-camera Sean = idealized Sean 10 years forward. James Spader energy, extreme certainty, unmoved by criticism. "Elon Musk if he was cool." Character is fictional. Do not conflate on-camera LESARUSS with real-life Sean A. Russell.

## Overlap With Other Brands
- **Sean A. Russell** — Mogul is SAR's primary video asset. Every tour date bridges back to the personal brand.
- **From Blur to Blueprint** — the origin story. Mogul picks up where FBTB closes. Book carries the past, Mogul carries the present.
- **Blueprint to Beyond** — the living daily series, companion narrative. Mogul is the camera version, B2B is the written version.
- **The Blueprint (sitcom)** — parallel sibling, not overlap. Mogul = live-action reality, Blueprint = animated scripted. Cross-pollinate later (Easter eggs, cameos), structurally separate in Phase 1.
- **Vegans Explore** — VE cities are tour stops. Every trip activates local VE chapters, captures member stories.
- **LESARUSS Platform** — trips also cover LESARUSS/Structured tier clients. Mogul is proof.
- **Every touring LESARUSS brand** — the Line Producer Console model locked here is the master touring standard. Chester, Shamanic Resin, Mad Tings, GeekFon, podcast network all adopt it with their own cartridge.
